About Kayla's Desserts

📸

Welcome to Kayla's Desserts! Every treat is homemade with love right here in the Sacramento area. From rich, gooey cookies to beautifully layered dessert cups and custom cakes, each item is baked fresh to order using quality ingredients you can taste in every bite.

What started as a passion for baking has grown into something truly special. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, treating yourself after a long week, or surprising someone you love, Kayla's Desserts is here to make every moment a little sweeter.

All orders require 24-hour advance notice so everything is made fresh just for you!
